In the human_interaction_partners folder you will find 3 different human proteins which have a direct relationship with CoV-proteins. It is possible that more proteins will be added in the future. If you want more information about the interactome, we suggest you this article: Yadi Zhou et al, Network-based drug repurposing for novel coronavirus 2019-nCoV/SARS-CoV-2, Cell Discovery, 16.03.2020 
(https://www.nature.com/articles/s41421-020-0153-3).
You can find the Sequence in FASTA-format in sequence_info.fasta for each Protein.
